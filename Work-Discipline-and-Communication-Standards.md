## 1. Daily Planning & Updates

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Daily Plan (Morning Update)** | Share your work plan before starting your day, including tasks and priorities |
| **Progress Updates** | Provide periodic updates during the day, especially for critical tasks, dependencies, or when priorities shift |
| **EOD (End of Day) Report** | Share a summary of what you completed, what is in progress, and any blockers |
| **Task Tracking** | Continuously update the status of assigned tasks (tickets/PRs/boards) |
| **Next Day Planning** | Confirm the next day's planned tasks before EOD today |

### Purpose

- Maintain clarity and alignment across projects
- Ensure transparency, ownership, and accountability
- Reduce delays caused by miscommunication
- Enable proactive support and planning

## 2. Work Hours & Attendance

| Requirement | Policy |
|-------------|--------|
| **Work Start Time** | Between 9:30 AM – 10:00 AM IST |
| **Minimum Work Duration** | 9 hours 30 minutes before clock-out |
| **Leaves During Initial Period** | No leave permitted during first 3 months of onboarding, except genuine emergencies (proof / reason may be requested) |
| **Attendance Responsibility** | It is the employee's responsibility to manage login/logout and ensure minimum hours are met |

### Purpose

- Maintain team availability alignment
- Build discipline and reliability
- Support consistent collaboration windows

---

## 3. Leave & Permission Rules

| Type | Policy |
|------|--------|
| **Planned Leave** | Must be informed at least 1–2 weeks in advance for approval |
| **Same-Day Leave / Emergency** | Inform as early as possible and communicate reason clearly |
| **Short Permission (1–2 hours)** | Inform the team lead or in the group at least 1–2 hours in advance |
| **Unapproved or Silent Absence** | Considered non-compliant and will be recorded |

---

## 4. Communication Expectations

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Ask questions early** | Do not wait until the end of day if stuck |
| **Use shared channels** | Ask questions in the group so others can help and stay informed |
| **Dependency Communication** | If blocked or waiting on someone, communicate clearly with details |
| **Documentation Check** | Review available docs/resources before asking repetitive questions |
| **Professional & respectful tone** | Maintain clarity and constructive communication at all times |

---

## 5. Responsibility & Ownership

- ✅ Take ownership of the tasks assigned to you
- ✅ Commit to timelines and deliver within planned schedules
- ✅ Communicate proactively if risks arise
- ✅ Avoid silence—communicate progress even if small
- ✅ Finish what you start; avoid leaving tasks halfway without context

---

## 6. Expected Behavior & Work Culture

- Be reliable, consistent, and punctual
- Take initiative instead of waiting for instructions
- Actively participate in team discussions and improvements
- Respect others' time and effort
- Support teammates when needed

---

## Why These Guidelines Matter

| Benefit | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **Improves productivity** | Efficiency for everyone |
| **Builds trust** | Transparency across teams |
| **Helps leadership** | Plan workloads and releases accurately |
| **Reduces crisis** | Avoids last-minute issues and unclear expectations |
| **Ensures fairness** | Professionalism in the workplace |

---

> **Remember:** These guidelines are not about micromanagement—they're about creating a healthy, transparent, and efficient work environment where everyone can thrive.
